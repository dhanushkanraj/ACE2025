How It Works

1. Booking Process

Start by booking your shipment through our online platform. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in or create an account on our website.
  2. Enter shipment details such as origin, destination, and cargo type.
  3. Select a preferred service option and provide necessary documents.
  4. Confirm the booking and make the payment.

2. Shipment Tracking

Once your shipment is booked, you can track its progress with these steps:

  • Log in to your account.
  • Go to the 'Track Shipment' section.
  • Enter your shipment reference number to view real-time updates.

3. Customs Procedures

We handle all customs-related tasks to ensure smooth international shipping. Here's what we do:

  • Prepare and submit all required customs documentation.
  • Ensure compliance with all international trade regulations.
  • Communicate with customs authorities to resolve any issues.
